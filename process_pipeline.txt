The data we have: 
point cloud (.ply) of femur and tibia, respective one layer (innen layer) and two layers (innen and outer layer)
data path: ...\SSM_femur_and_tibia-main\aussen_femur --> two layers femur
           ...\SSM_femur_and_tibia-main\aussen_tibia --> two layers tibia
           ...\SSM_femur_and_tibia-main\innen_femur  --> one layer femur
           ...\SSM_femur_and_tibia-main\innen_tibia  --> one layer tibia

Step 1: segment innen and outer layer, remove the outliers, then downsample the clean innen and outer layer respectively
-----run cut_innen.m, the clean downsampled bones(.ply) are saved in:
           ...\SSM_femur_and_tibia-main\dSampOut_femur\r --> outer layer right femur 
           ...\SSM_femur_and_tibia-main\dSampOut_femur\l --> outer layer left femur 
           ...\SSM_femur_and_tibia-main\dSampIn_femur\r --> innen layer right femur 
           ...\SSM_femur_and_tibia-main\dSampIn_femur\l --> innen layer left femur 
           ...\SSM_femur_and_tibia-main\dSampOut_tibia\r --> outer layer right tibia
           ...\SSM_femur_and_tibia-main\dSampOut_tibia\l --> outer layer left tibia
           ...\SSM_femur_and_tibia-main\dSampIn_tibia\r --> innen layer right tibia
           ...\SSM_femur_and_tibia-main\dSampIn_tibia\l --> innen layer left tibia

Step 2: prealignment using TMM, cut off around half of the bone, bring the rest half to TMM, then do Principal Component Analysis and show SSM results
-----run SSMSingleLayers.m
TMM could be applied in two different ways: 'with scale' or 'without scale', so we have 8 scenarios in total:
1. femur, outer later, with scale
2. femur, outer later, without scale
3. femur, innen later, with scale
4. femur, innen later, without scale
5. tibia, outer later, with scale
6. tibia, outer later, without scale
7. tibia, innen later, with scale
8. tibia, innen later, without scale
statistical results of PCA, visualization of SSM, and figures during the pipeline are saved in:
           ...\SSM_femur_and_tibia-main\results
See detailed infomation from result_map.txt, path: ...\SSM_femur_and_tibia-main\results\result_map.txt
